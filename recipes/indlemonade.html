Limbu Pani!

Description:

This is a basic Lemonade paired with Indian twists along the way, that's it a basic Indian Lemonade!

Ingredients:

  • ½ Cup freshly squeezed lemon or lime juice
  • 4 Cups water
  • 5 Tablespoons sugar
  • ¾ Teaspoon kala namak (black salt)
  • ½ Teaspoon roasted cumin powder
  • ¼ Teaspoon salt
  • ¼ Teaspoon amchur
  • ⅛ Teaspoon black pepper
  • Mint leaves garnish
  • Ice cubes

Steps:

  1. Combine the lemon juice, water, sugar and spices together in a pitcher. Taste and adjust sugar if needed.
  2. Serve over ice and garnish with mint.
